Pitt PokeResearch

User Guide

For iOS and Android

You will need a Google Account to Add Markers. Viewing does not require a login.

To Refresh Map Data: Click the Refresh icon on the bottom right

To Add a Marker: Long press on the map. I suggest doing this as zoomed in as you can.

iOS

You will find two icons on the botom right. Once is to refresh map data, one is to find your location.

To

You will have to bypass any security warnings since this is not yet in the Play Store. This is beta software and over time may have errors.

If you wish to test functionality, place test markers onto the map.

The software is limited to within 50 miles of Pittsburgh, PA for reasons of software scale.
